Stock Analysis
What do I do here?

On this portion of the website, you can now search up the stock you want to view and our stock analysis program will give you an analysis of your selected stock. The program will factor in many things about your selected stock such as new product releases, what they are selling, who their audience and much more! The best part is the analysis is free!

How do I get an analysis of a stock
To get an analysis of a stock, enter a stock ticker and select a category. Then click the run button and wait for the program to give you an analysis
